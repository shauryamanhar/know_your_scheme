Where to stay

  1. Information on Manali and Keylong

    • Share This
    • Rating

    Get information about Manali and Keylong provided by Himachal Pradesh Tourism Development Corporation (HPTDC). Users can get details regarding access, climate, tourist places, hotels, club houses, sightseeing tours, etc. in Manali as well as Keylong. Details of hotel tariff, address, location and link to online booking are also available.

  2. Information about hotels in Meghalaya

    • Share This
    • Rating

    Get information about private and government hotels in Meghalaya. Details about the hotel, location, address and contact details are provided. Users can get information about hotels in Shillong, Sohra, Ri Bhoi, Jowai, Tura, Williamnagar and Baghmara.

  3. Information on hotels in Andaman & Nicobar Islands

    • Share This
    • Rating

    Get information about accommodation facilities available in Andaman & Nicobar Island. Users can get details related to government accommodation, three star hotels, guest houses, youth hostels, home stay and bed and breakfast establishments. Information about accommodation type, size, tariff, contact details, etc. is provided.

  4. Information on hotels in Ajmer and Pushkar of Rajasthan

    • Share This
    • Rating

    Get information about hotels in Ajmer and Pushkar by Tourism Department of Rajasthan. Users can get list of heritage, equivalent to heritage hotel, mid range and top end hotels with their addresses and phone numbers. Information about Ajmer Pushkar, its climate, sightseeing, access, transportation, paying guest accommodation, etc. can also be accessed.

  5. List of approved heritage hotels

    • Share This
    • Rating

    A list of approved heritage hotels in India is provided by the Ministry of Tourism. Users can get details about hotels, their addresses, phone numbers and email ids. Information on number of rooms available in the heritage hotels is also given.

  6. Information on hotels by Bihar State Tourism Development Corporation

    • Share This
    • Rating

    Find information about the hotel facilities provided by the Bihar State Tourism Development Corporation (BSTDC). Details of hotels and tourist bungalows at Patna, Sonepur, Nalanda, Vaishali, Bodhgaya, Rajgir, Muzaffarpur, Sasaram, Mohania, Saharsa, Purnea, etc. are available. Users can get information like name of the hotel, type of rooms, facilities, distance from airport and railway station...

  7. Hotels and lodges in Belgaum district of Karnataka

    • Share This
    • Rating

    Get information about accommodation available in Belgaum District of Karnataka. Users can get contact details of hotels, lodges and restaurants with their address and contact numbers.

  8. Paying guest accommodation in Ajmer and Pushkar of Rajasthan

    • Share This
    • Rating

    Get information about paying guest accommodation in Ajmer and Pushkar by Rajasthan Tourism Department. Users can get details about paying guest accommodation, address of house owner, his profession and hobbies, name of contact person, e-mail address, types of rooms, number of beds, expected tariff and services available. Information about Ajmer and Pushkar, its climate, sightseeing, access,...

  9. Information on accommodation in Tripura

    • Share This
    • Rating

    Get information about accommodation and modes of transports to reach Tripura. Users can get details regarding air, rail and bus services available to reach Tripura. Information on government accommodation, private hotels and their tariffs is also available.

  10. Check details of registered hotels in Himachal Pradesh

    • Share This
    • Rating

    Check information related to hotels in various districts of Himachal Pradesh. Contact information of hotels, their location, total number of rooms and tariff are also provided.

  11. Telephone directory for booking accommodation by Ecotourism Society of Himachal Pradesh

    • Share This
    • Rating

    You can find the telephone directory for booking accommodation provided by the Eco-Tourism Society of Himachal Pradesh. Users can find details such as name of the officials, designations, their contact numbers, Fax numbers, and email ids etc.
